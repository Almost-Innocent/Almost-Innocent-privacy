How to delete your data

  1. Delete cloud saves yourself
    Open Google Play Games → Profile/Settings → Manage cloud saves and delete the entries for <APP-NAME>.
    Alternatively, manage Play Games data in your Google Account and remove the saves for this app there.
  2. Request deletion of questionnaire data
    Send us a short email from the address you used in the questionnaire to frontendprojektarbeit@gmail.com.
    Please include (if known) the approximate submission date. We delete within 30 days and confirm by email.

What might be retained?

Only data required by law (e.g., statutory retention) may be kept for the mandated period; everything else will be deleted.
